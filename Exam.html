GATE

The Graduate Aptitude Test in Engineering (GATE) examination is one of the most competitive examinations in India. It’s used to test the understanding of various undergraduate subjects in engineering and science. If you want to secure a job in Maharatnas and Navaratnas Companies after graduating from engineering, then you have to write the GATE Exam. Companies like BPCL, IOCL, ONGC, BHEL, NTPC, etc. are some of the dream companies to work in India. These companies will provide good salaries like a government organization and also provide job security. GATE exam is one of the most important competitive exams after graduation engineering.

Eligibility Criteria to apply and write GATE Exam:
Nationality: Applicant should be an Indian National to apply for GATE 2021. Foreign candidates who have citizenship of Sri Lanka, Singapore, Ethiopia, Nepal, Bangladesh, and the United Arab Emirates (UAE) are also eligible to apply for the GATE exam
Educational qualification: The candidates must have completed or must be in their final year of Bachelor’s degree in Engineering/Technology or Master’s degree in Science in any relevant subject

Age Limit: There is no age limit for GATE Exam
A number of attempts: There is no restriction on the number of times a candidate can appear for the GATE Exams.

If you want to secure a bank job in the Reserve Bank of India after graduating from engineering, then RBI Grade B Examination is the best choice. Once you enter into RBI Career, then you will have good growth prospects if you are recruited at a young age. Along with this, you can be promoted to be a deputy governor after promotions through RBI departmental exams.
Eligibility criteria to apply and write RBI Grade B exam:
Nationality: An Indian, Bhutan citizen, Nepal, or any refugee, from Tibet who has immigrated before01st January 1962. Also, people with Indian origin who have migrated from places including Burma, Pakistan, Sri Lanka, East African countries of Uganda/ Kenya/ United Republic of Tanzania/ Malawi/ Zaire/ Ethiopia/ Zambia/ Vietnam to get settled in India
Educational qualification: Minimum of 60% aggregate marks (while 50% for specific categories) along with 12th (or diploma or anything equivalent as such) and the marks obtained in 10th examinations

Age Limit: If you applying for the position of RBI officer Grade B, then age must be between 21 years to 30 years
A number of attempts: Candidates can attempt for a maximum of 6 times. No such restriction if a candidate comes under particular categories including SC/ ST/ PWD
